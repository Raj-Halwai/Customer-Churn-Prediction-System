# Customer Churn Prediction System Using Machine Learning & Power BI

## Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges faced by subscription-based businesses. Retaining existing customers is significantly more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. This project aims to identify customers who are likely to discontinue services and provide actionable business insights to improve customer retention.

The project combines Data Analytics, Machine Learning, and Business Intelligence to build an end-to-end churn prediction solution. Using the IBM Telco Customer Churn dataset, the data was cleaned, analyzed, and transformed into meaningful business insights through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), predictive modeling, and interactive Power BI dashboards.

## Objectives

* Predict customer churn using Machine Learning algorithms.
* Identify key factors influencing customer attrition.
* Analyze customer behavior and revenue impact.
* Develop interactive dashboards for business stakeholders.
* Recommend retention strategies to reduce customer churn.

## Dataset

* Source: IBM Telco Customer Churn Dataset
* Records: 7,000+ customers
* Features: Customer demographics, contract information, internet services, payment methods, monthly charges, tenure, and churn status.
* Target Variable: Churn (Yes/No)

## Key Insights

* Month-to-Month customers exhibit the highest churn rates.
* Fiber Optic users show significantly higher churn compared to other internet service categories.
* Electronic Check customers are more likely to churn than customers using automatic payment methods.
* Long-term contract customers generate the highest revenue and demonstrate stronger customer loyalty.
* Customer churn contributes to substantial revenue loss, emphasizing the importance of proactive retention strategies.

## Business Recommendations

* Focus retention campaigns on customers with tenure below 12 months.
* Encourage Month-to-Month customers to switch to annual contracts.
* Provide loyalty incentives for Fiber Optic subscribers.
* Monitor customers with high monthly charges.
* Implement predictive monitoring to identify customers at risk of churn.
